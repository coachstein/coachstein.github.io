Bio

My professional experience has been an amazing journey through mechanical & electrical design for the automated machine industry using Autodesk products. After I left the machine design industry I moved into consulting for an Autodesk reseller where I spent most of my time working with AutoCAD Electrical, managing software development projects with a multi-geographical development team, and working with clients on process improvements.

After leaving the reseller I went back to industry as a CAD / PDM manager for a global company with hundreds of users that I was in charge of making sure their CAD / PDM systems were always on always available. I managed a team of interns that wrote customizations for our systems.

I started at Autodesk in consulting and moved through the ranks becoming a senior consulting manager within our consulting organization. I then moved into Partner Enablement to help build that program out. Now I am a Chief Product Owner within the Fusion product line focssed on Data Management.
